T-2
    “Dying,” Morrie suddenly said, “is only one thing to be sad over, Mitch.
    Living unhappily is something else. So many of the people who come to visit me are unhappy.” Why?
    “Well, for one thing, the culture we have does not make people feel good about themselves.
    We’re teaching the wrong things. And you have to be strong enough to say if the culture doesn’t work,
    don’t buy it. Create your own. Most people can’t do it.
    They’re more unhappy than me—even in my current condition.
    “I may be dying, but I am surrounded by loving, caring souls. How many people can say that?”
